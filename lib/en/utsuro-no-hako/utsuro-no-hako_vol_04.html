"…What's the matter, Kazuki?" Maria questions, her eyes round at my odd behavior.

"Maria… Why did you leave before me without saying anything?"

"…I left early because I'm not a fan of Mogi; you know that. Why is your face so pale? And why are you here so early, too? You don't need to talk to

Mogi anymore?"

"No, it's fine." I tell her:

"You're the most important person to me." Maria's eyes open even wider- -but then quickly narrow.

"I see."

She then strokes my head gently.

"I swear… You've been different recently." Maria's caught on to the changes in me a little bit.

"It's all in your head."

So I play it off as nothing.

As payback for Maria's head pats, I run my fingers through her long hair.

The shy smile rising on her face still contains a hint of melancholy to me, and yeah-it's frustrating.
