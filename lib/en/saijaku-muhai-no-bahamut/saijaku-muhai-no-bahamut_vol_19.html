But‐Fugil who had been pretending to be unable to fight as a Drag-Knight until then eradicated the imperial soldiers in that place and told Lux about his mission to change the world using the ArtifactVariable Drag-Ride

Humans would rule over other humans and gradually form a tyrannical system‐and like that the balance of the world would gradually go out of order. Fugil had been secretly maneuvering behind the scenes for more than a thousand years in order to correct the crumbling balance.

No, Lux didn't know whether Fugil's deeds could really be called as secret maneuvers.

Fugil was genuinely wishing to save this world.

He would save people. And then the successors of those people he saved would eventually grow impudent and prideful, and begin to oppress the people once more. When that time came, Fugil would act as the punisher who destroyed the system that went out of order.

He told Lux to massacre all the important people in the Arcadia Empire, the imperial family and the chief vassals to bring an end to everything.

People wouldn't change their way of thinking once they had been infected by the poison called authority.

He declared that Lux's method that wished to urge such people to reform themselves wouldn't be able to change anything.

But Lux didn't give in even after being told that.

"Even so, I‐"

Lux looked back and thought.

Within the Old Empire, Lux and Airi didn't get infected by the poison of authority and were kept at a distance by the people in power. In such situation‐they were saved by Fugil.

Without Fugil's strength, Lux wouldn't even be able to stand on the stage to assert his will like this.

Once the people who didn't know anything one-sidedly decided that Lux was evil just because he was royalty and hated him. They even abandoned his mother to die. It made Lux hate the world.

But, now after Philuffy had saved him, he couldn't bring himself to hate the people.

Fugil was saying that the Arcadia family and the people who sided with them, not even one of them held compassion toward the masses. But‐

"I believe, that you are wrong."

"Hou……"

The duty of a hero was to ascertain what is evil and destroying it without fail.

Fugil claimed that he had continued to destroy such evils until now. In response Lux looked straight into his eyes.

"You shouldn't just leave it to other people, you should lead them yourself!

If you have the ideal that you want people to understand each other then‐!"

"But, you are unable to turn your ideals into reality."

Fugil smiled scornfully towards Lux and replied.

Lux and Count Atismata were outwitted by the emperor. Their revolution ended in failure.

"You are unable to judge evil and save the good. To do such thing is impossible even with my power. A power that equaled a god would be necessary for that. You don't have both the resolve and the qualification to control such power. You are the weakest who can't even ascertain what should be saved and what should be discarded. Someone like you‐"

After Fugil muttered that, Uroboros squirmed behind him and emitted sevencolored light radiantly.

Right after that, the two of them drew out their sword as though mirroring each other and rushed forward.

They walked through the long stone corridor toward the terrace with the Triad following them behind as guards.

Lux noticed something important through the looping parade and the battle against Fugil for Avalon.

Lux‐couldn't become a hero on his own.

To see someone as hero meant pushing one's own ideal to that person.

"…………"

Lux saw the blue sky that was expanding at the other side of the corridor's handrail and thought.

Fugil and Arshalia, they tried to become the hero and goddess to answer the expectation that was placed on them.

They aimed to become an existence that couldn't possibly be reached as long as they stayed human. Even so they continued to fight.

They were cornered by great ideal and dream that couldn't be shouldered by human's hand‐and got lost in a labyrinth.

After slowly walking through the corridor for a while, he saw a familiar blue haired girl standing ahead.

Krulcifer who was wearing a dress saw Lux and smiled.

"As I thought, you look good in that attire‐"

"Don't flatter me too much. Even now I'm forcing myself a lot after all."

Lux was feeling pressure from the girl's praise. Then,

"It's fine if you don't force yourself. We will support you if there is any trouble. That's why‐"

"Yep. Rest assured, Lu-chan."

Celis who was wearing a military uniform was encouraging him with a strong and kind voice.

Beside her, his childhood friend was also giving him a faint smile.

"Thank you. Celis-senpai, Phi-chan."

Yoruka who was wearing her usual black outfit was also standing beside the stair.

"Good luck Aruji-sama. I too shall accompany you even until the depth of hell, until your wish is realized."

"That's reassuring but……. Don't cause any ruckus in the ceremony okay?"

"I'll strive to do so."

Yoruka smiled guilelessly toward him. She was just like usual as expected.

After receiving the encouragement from the four, Lux went up the stairs with Airi and the others.

(The peace of the new kingdom‐I can't shoulder such heavy burden by myself. I can't‐save the whole world by myself.)

What he accomplished was possible only because Lisha and the others, everyone was there for him.

If they weren't there for him, he couldn't possibly accomplish it.

That was why‐he didn't intend to shoulder the burden by himself even from here on.

He went up the stairs and came out to the open terrace of the palace.

When he showed his face to the populace, the honored guests from other countries, and the nobles who gathered here, very loud cheers were raised.

There, Lisha who was wearing a red dress and Celis's father, the head of one of the four great nobles, Dist Ralgris were already waiting.

"Then, I shall introduce……the new king who will lead the new kingdom for this one year. ‐King Lux."

*WAaaaaAAAAAA……!*

Cheers so loud it could burst the eardrums were reverberating in the whole area like a ripple.

Lux watched the populace with a calm gaze and slowly waved his hand.

"It will only be for a year, but I will serve as the king of the new kingdom during that time. I will have to rely on Lord Dist's assistance in regard to the political matter, but I intend to play my role to the best of my ability in order to improve the new kingdom to become an even better country."

From now on Lux would still be a student in the academy while also taking up the role as the king at the same time. This was decided from the discussion with the four great nobles and the consuls.

Currently the central figures of the new kingdom were gone because of various incidents and accidents. Lisha's presence alone would be lacking to unite the populace.

A figure with strong unifying force was necessary to bring together the worried populace.

In this one year Lux had raised many accomplishments, got his criminal collar removed, and the populace also came to recognize him. If it was Lux, he wasn't lacking anything in that point.

Of course, in the end Lux was only king at the surface. Most of the governing would be carried out by Lord Dist of the four great nobles but‐even then he was undoubtedly a king.

From here on an effort to switch the governing system to republicanism would begin. This role would serve as a bridge in that effort.

In order to spread the modest peace that he wished for, he decided to walk toward that ideal together with the girls who fought with him and stayed at his side.

"I am‐not that strong. I cannot become a hero who can save everything in this world. However……"

He exchanged glance with Lisha and Airi. The two of them returned a heated gaze toward him.

His courage welled up seeing that.

"I wish that more and more people can find something that only you yourself can do. I wish to spread the will for everyone to help each other to even more people."

Just like how Philuffy saved him from the loneliness when she was a child.

He wouldn't try to be a hero who wanted to save every single people in need, but someone who could show others the path to do so themselves as much as possible.

"I think there will be time where I stumble due to my lack of strength. I think there'll be also times where I encounter misfortune and toyed by fate so that things don't go well. But‐"

Lux cut off his words and looked over the populace before declaring.

"My friends from the academy who has been supporting me all this time, my experiences from everything I had gone through, they have taught me. That we can become happy by overcoming those hardships. That's why, I wish to use this one year to think of the way to create such country."

The people were listening to Lux's speech with a quiet expression.

Before long small applauses could be heard here and there before they became a loud cheer that reverberated through the spring's blue sky.
